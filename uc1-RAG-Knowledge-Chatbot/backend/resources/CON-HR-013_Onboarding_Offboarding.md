# CON-HR-013 — Employee Onboarding & Offboarding Procedures

**Company:** Contoso Corp  
**Division:** Human Resources — People Operations  
**Policy ID:** CON-HR-013  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Reviewed:** November 2023  
**Policy Owner:** VP, People Operations  
**Applies To:** All Regular and Fixed-Term Employees  

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy documents the standard procedures for welcoming new employees into Contoso Corp and for managing the departure of employees professionally, securely, and in compliance with legal requirements. A consistent onboarding experience accelerates time-to-productivity and reinforces our culture; a well-executed offboarding process protects company assets and preserves relationships.

---

## 2. Pre-Hire Onboarding (Before Day 1)

### 2.1 Hiring Manager Responsibilities (T-10 to T-1 Business Days Before Start)

| Action | Timing | Owner |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Submit IT equipment request via IT Portal | T-10 days | Manager |
| Complete New Hire Setup Form in Workday (role, team, cost center) | T-10 days | Manager |
| Assign onboarding buddy (Grade 5+ peer) | T-7 days | Manager |
| Schedule Day 1 agenda and first-week check-ins | T-5 days | Manager |
| Notify team of new hire's start, role, and background | T-3 days | Manager |
| Ensure workspace (desk, office supplies) or remote setup is ready | T-1 day | Manager + Facilities |

### 2.2 HR Responsibilities

- Issue welcome email with Day 1 instructions, parking/building access info, and Workday pre-boarding link (T-7 days).
- Process I-9 documentation: electronic via remote I-9 vendor (HireRight) for remote hires; in-person on Day 1 for on-site hires.
- Enroll employee in background check tracking; confirm clearance before start date.
- Set up Workday profile, benefits enrollment, and payroll.

### 2.3 New Employee Pre-Boarding (via Workday)

New hires can complete the following before Day 1:
- Review and e-sign offer letter, confidentiality agreement, and employment policies.
- Set up direct deposit.
- Complete emergency contact and tax withholding forms.
- Review the welcome video from the CEO.
- Access the virtual new hire guide at **onboarding.contosocorp.com**.

## 4. First 30 Days — Structured Onboarding Program

### Required Training (Must Be Completed Within 30 Days)

1. Contoso Corp Onboarding Curriculum (Workday Learning — 6 modules, ~4 hours)
2. Code of Conduct & Ethics (Workday — 45 minutes)
3. Data Privacy & Security Awareness (Workday — 60 minutes)
4. Anti-Harassment Training (Workday — 90 minutes)
5. Benefits enrollment (must be completed within 30 days of hire for coverage to begin)

### Check-Ins

| Milestone | Meeting | Participants |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Day 5 | First-week check-in | Employee + Manager |
| Day 14 | Two-week pulse | Employee + HR BP |
| Day 30 | 30-day review: progress on onboarding plan, initial feedback | Employee + Manager + HR BP |
| Day 90 | End of introductory period review | Employee + Manager |

The 90-day review determines continuation of employment. It is not a formal performance review but a structured conversation about fit, expectations, and development.

## 5. Onboarding Buddy Program

Every new employee at Grade 5 and above is assigned an onboarding buddy — a peer from the same team or department who is not their direct manager. Buddy responsibilities:

- Meet with the new hire at least weekly for the first 30 days.
- Be the "go-to" for cultural and informal questions ("How do I request a meeting room?" / "What's the lunch situation?").
- Introduce the new hire to key colleagues outside the immediate team.
- Provide feedback to HR after 30 days on the new hire's integration.

Buddies are compensated with 4 Kudos points per completed 30-day engagement (redeemable in the Workday recognition catalog).

## 6. Offboarding — Employee Resignation or Retirement

### 6.1 Notice & Transition Planning

- Minimum notice expected: 2 weeks (all roles); 4 weeks strongly encouraged for management and technical leadership.
- Manager and employee agree on a transition plan within 2 business days of notice, covering: project handoffs, documentation, and knowledge transfer.
- HR schedules an exit interview within the final week.

### 6.2 Separation Checklist (Employee)

The following must be completed on or before the last day:

- [ ] Return all company equipment (laptop, phone, access badges, keys, corporate credit card)
- [ ] Transfer ownership of all work files to manager or designated successor
- [ ] Complete all pending expense reports in Concur
- [ ] Delete company accounts from personal devices (email, MDM unenrollment)
- [ ] Respond to HR exit interview (voluntary but strongly encouraged)
- [ ] Sign separation agreement if applicable (required for severance eligibility)

### 6.3 IT Offboarding (Same Day as Termination)

IT will automatically execute the following at end of business on the last day (or immediately for involuntary separations):

- Disable Azure AD account and all SSO-dependent access.
- Revoke VPN access.
- Forward email to manager for 30 days.
- Preserve mailbox and files per data retention schedule (CON-IT-004).
- Unenroll personal devices from MDM.

### 6.4 Final Pay

- Final pay is issued per applicable state law (e.g., California: final day for involuntary; next regular payday for voluntary).
- Accrued but unused PTO is paid out per state law and company policy (CON-HR-002).
- Any outstanding advances, overpayments, or equipment charges are deducted with employee consent; legal garnishment where consent is not given.

## 7. Involuntary Termination Procedures

Involuntary terminations are handled by HR in coordination with the manager. Key requirements:

- HR must be involved in all involuntary termination conversations.
- A separation script and documentation package must be prepared by HR before the meeting.
- IT access is revoked simultaneously with the termination notification.
- The employee is accompanied by HR or Security to collect personal belongings.
- Severance eligibility is determined per CON-HR-012 (Severance Policy).
- COBRA and benefits continuation information is provided within 14 days per federal law.

## 8. Alumni Program

Contoso Corp alumni in good standing (departed without cause) are eligible for the Contoso Corp Alumni Network:

- Access to the alumni LinkedIn group and annual alumni event.
- Eligibility for rehire (subject to role availability and HR review) after a minimum 6-month break.
- 20% discount on Contoso Corp training programs and certifications for 2 years post-departure.
