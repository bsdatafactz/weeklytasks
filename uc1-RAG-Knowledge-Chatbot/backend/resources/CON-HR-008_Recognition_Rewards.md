# CON-HR-008 — Employee Recognition & Rewards Policy

**Company:** Contoso Corp  
**Division:** Human Resources — Total Rewards  
**Policy ID:** CON-HR-008  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Reviewed:** October 2023  
**Policy Owner:** VP, Total Rewards  
**Applies To:** All Regular Employees  

---

## 1. Purpose

Contoso Corp believes that timely, meaningful recognition is a powerful driver of employee engagement and retention. This policy governs formal and informal recognition programs, establishes guidelines for gifts and awards, and ensures equitable and tax-compliant recognition practices.

---

## 2. Recognition Programs

### 2.1 Peer-to-Peer Recognition — "Kudos"

Any employee may send a Kudos recognition to a colleague through Workday at any time. Kudos are tied to one of Contoso Corp's five core values and include a written message. There is no monetary component. Kudos are visible on the employee's Workday profile and may be referenced in performance discussions.

**Guidelines:**
- Be specific — describe the behavior, not just the outcome.
- Kudos should be genuine; sending Kudos as social reciprocation undermines the program.
- Managers are encouraged to send at least 2 Kudos per team member per quarter.

### 2.2 Manager Spot Awards

Managers may award spot bonuses to recognize exceptional one-time contributions (see CON-HR-004 Section 5.2 for amounts). In addition to cash spot awards, managers may award:

| Award Type | Value | Approval Required |
|-----------|-------|-------------------|
| Gift card (company vendor) | Up to $100 | Manager |
| Extra PTO day (1 day) | $0 cash | Manager + HR notification |
| Team celebration lunch/dinner | Up to $50/person | Manager |
| Cash spot bonus | $500 – $5,000 | Manager + VP |

All non-cash awards with a value over $50 are taxable and reported on the employee's W-2.

### 2.3 Quarterly "Cornerstone Awards"

Each quarter, each business unit nominates one employee per 50-person headcount for a Cornerstone Award, recognizing sustained exceptional contribution. Nominations are reviewed by a cross-functional panel. Winners receive:

- $1,000 cash award (taxable)
- Certificate signed by the CEO
- Feature in the Contoso Corp internal newsletter
- Invitation to the Cornerstone Gala (annual event, Q1)

Employees on a PIP or with an active disciplinary action are not eligible.

### 2.4 Annual "Excellence Awards"

At the annual All-Hands event, Contoso Corp presents Excellence Awards in the following categories:

| Award | Description | Prize |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| Innovation of the Year | Breakthrough solution with measurable business impact | $5,000 + trophy |
| Client Champion | Exceptional client satisfaction and relationship impact | $3,000 + trophy |
| Culture Carrier | Embodies Contoso Corp values; lifts team culture | $3,000 + trophy |
| Rising Star | Outstanding performance in first 2 years at Contoso Corp | $2,000 + trophy |
| Team of the Year | Collective achievement of a cross-functional team | $2,000/member + plaque |

Awards are nominated by any employee and selected by the Executive Leadership Team.

### 2.5 Service Milestone Awards

Contoso Corp recognizes employees for years of service at the following milestones:

| Milestone | Recognition |
|-----------|-------------|
| 1 year | Digital Kudos from manager + company swag kit |
| 3 years | $250 gift card |
| 5 years | $500 gift card + choice of commemorative item |
| 10 years | $1,000 gift card + personalized plaque + 1 extra PTO day |
| 15 years | $1,500 gift card + executive lunch + 2 extra PTO days |
| 20 years | $2,500 gift card + sabbatical eligibility (4 weeks paid) |
| 25 years | $5,000 gift card + executive recognition event |

Service awards are automatically triggered by Workday on the anniversary date. HR coordinates fulfillment.

## 3. Receiving Gifts from External Parties

Per the Code of Conduct (CON-HR-001), employees may not accept gifts from vendors, clients, or other external parties exceeding USD $100 in value per calendar year from a single source. Gifts received must be:

- Disclosed to your manager if they exceed $50 in value.
- Reported to the Ethics team via the gift disclosure form if they exceed $100.
- Returned or donated if they exceed the $100 threshold and cannot be shared with the team.

Seasonal gift baskets, branded merchandise, and meals within meal policy limits do not require disclosure. Gifts of cash or cash equivalents (gift cards, checks) must always be declined or returned.

---

## 4. Giving Gifts to External Parties

Gifts to clients, partners, or other external parties may only be given with manager approval and must comply with the recipient's own gift policy, which employees are responsible for checking. Gifts to government employees or officials are subject to strict restrictions — contact Legal before proceeding. All gifts to external parties must be expensed through Concur with a documented business purpose.

---

## 5. Tax Treatment

| Award Type | Tax Treatment |
|-----------|---------------|
| Cash bonuses | Taxable; subject to supplemental withholding |
| Gift cards | Taxable; grossed up by payroll |
| Non-cash awards over $50 | Taxable; reported on W-2 |
| Length-of-service awards up to $400 (tangible property) | Tax-exempt per IRS guidelines |
| Nominal items (< $25 de minimis) | Generally non-taxable |

Contoso Corp does not gross up income taxes on awards except where specifically stated. Employees with questions about the tax treatment of a specific award should consult their tax advisor.

---

## 6. Equity in Recognition

Contoso Corp's People Analytics team monitors recognition activity quarterly for patterns of inequity. Managers who show significant disparities in recognition frequency by demographic group will receive coaching from their HR Business Partner. All recognition decisions must be based on observable performance and behavior — not personal relationships, appearance, or protected characteristics.
